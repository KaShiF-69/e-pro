Indoor Cycling Programs

Challenge your body and ignite your energy with our high-intensity cycling experiences.

Beginner Spin

Perfect for first-timers! Learn bike setup, basic riding positions, and build cardiovascular strength at your own pace with motivating instructors.

Power Ride

A fast-paced interval ride focusing on endurance, resistance climbs, and calorie-burning bursts. Great for intermediate to advanced riders.

Rhythm Ride

Ride to the beat of high-energy playlists with choreographed moves on the bike. A full-body cardio party that blends fitness and fun.
